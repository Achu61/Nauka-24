About College

AICTE, New Delhi approved technical institution

AKNM Govt. Polytechnic College, Thirurangadi, established in 1994, is one of the well reputed AICTE, New Delhi approved technical institution in Malappuram District, under Government sector affiliated to the Directorate of Technical Education, Kerala state. The college is situated at Chelari, near to National highway 66 in Thirurangadi Taluk. The Campus spreads over 6.23 acres of land and offers 3 diploma courses in Electronics, Electronics & Communication and Computer Engineering branches. More than 525 students are studying in these different branches and about 60 staff (including ministerial) are also working in the institution in a serene atmosphere.
