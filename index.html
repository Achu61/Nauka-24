Hello Tech Enthusiasts,

Our fascination with different technologies are ever increasing. Applications are almost endless and the possibilities are becoming realities at an even faster pace. Come and enjoy The Inter College Tech-Fest of AKNM Government Polytechnic College NAUKA 24. Our bright young students will be showcasing their latest creations ranging from the most entertaining to the most practical designs for everyday life. And it is your turn too to exhibit your skills and compete with others. Get ready to win a variety of prizes.

Find out about us in history

The Nauka23 State-level Inter-college Technical Fest, held on February 24 and 25, 2023, showcased the exceptional skills of over 500 students from diverse polytechnic and engineering colleges. The event featured 10 technical competitions, ranging from debugging to circuit design, and 11 non-technical events, including cook without fire and treasure hunts. Students not only demonstrated their prowess but also presented innovative ideas through department stalls displaying Arduino projects and static models. The fest left a lasting impact with a grand finale featuring a project exhibition by the fashion designing department and a dynamic performance by artists like mallu rapper Fejo and playback singer Sony Mohan. Nauka23 was an unforgettable celebration of talent, creativity, and collaboration among students from various colleges.
